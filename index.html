Fixing the Engine of Innovation

Like an untwining strand of DNA, academia and the workplace are spiraling away from each other, resulting in overspecialized academics, undertrained employees, tremendous waste, and stunted growth in traditional industries.

The forward advancement of distributed ledger technology, in principle, relies on the same harmony of its own growth. DiscoverBlockchain aims to quickly bring these twin aspects back into synergistic operation.

×

Flexible Education for Disruptive Conditions

As universities are challenged to adapt their content to the new generation, that very same generation already understands what an agile learning environment would look like.

New models must be student-centric, open-source, choose-your-own-adventure style education, with nuanced guidelines ensuring arrival at the best ending: a mix of general savvy with targeted competencies.

DiscoverBlockchain Campus

The DiscoverBlockchain campus is a virtual platform which is a combination of a professional marketplace and think-tank that will house 2nd and 3rd generation Blockchain Technology projects and beyond.

Students can succeed as contractors, contractors can contribute as teachers, teachers can build as entrepreneurs, and entrepreneurs can learn as students - developing their history and network all the while.

Principles for Intuiting Competency

While enthusiasm for disruption and self-granted job titles are often necessary boxes for prospects to tick, there are several disparate qualifiers which, though intuitively valuable, are hard to demonstrate.

Identifying these factors and standardizing them can help us meet the need for at-a-glance methods for proving (or vetting) competency - without 3 month probations.

Universal Tenure

Qualification is not just what you know. Or who you know. Qualification is a constantly-updating consensus about you that is made by a decentralized, disparate whole.

An individual’s relative involvement with, or impact on, the global development of distributed ledger technology is approximated algorithmically, and given a DiscoverScore (DSCORE). DSCORE is a moving aggregate of an individual's diverse experiences, known collaborators, and gained reputation.

×

Decentralized Talent

In software and technology, there are trends in increasingly lateral solutions for education, recruitment, research and development. Businesses are becoming increasingly dependent on external services from other business and contractors, while at the same time the duration of these relationships are becoming shorter or more concise. The same is true of learning institutions, who are in tight competition with an increasingly open-source world where knowledge is quick and ubiquitous.

This has both caused and been caused by a shift in the demands of the talent as well. Learning and working online are gaining popularity, due to the dramatic increase in flexibility and opportunities available. Outside of in-person workshops and networking events, most Blockchain development occurs over the Internet, relying on the same strengths that are provided by remote learning and freelancing. In our solution, we integrate the remote learning and freelancing models into a single, reciprocal ecosystem.

What is DiscoverBlockchain?

DiscoverBlockchain is both an educational and professional marketplace helping clients and individuals connect with skilled Blockchain Technology educators, contractors and entrepreneurs. What differentiates us from the other freelance marketplaces is that our platform is specific to Blockchain Technology projects and purposes. Additionally, beyond conventional functions of the platform we apply the transparent and objective DiscoverSCORE validation algorithm: our chief differentiating value and unique application of Blockchain Technology.

Why does this project need Blockchain Technology?

While many are already hard at work building new protocols, DiscoverBlockchain has recognized the ‘self-disruptive’ aspect of the technology demands attention. There exists a necessity for the educational and professional marketplace infrastructure that will help the building process become more reliable, efficient, transparent and secure. Without these, the technology will continue to struggle to launch, destabilized by the weight of its own immense potential. We want people to have control of their educational and professional profiles in a decentralized, peer-to-peer environment using our involvement score (DSCore) and determine the path they choose to take with their skills.

Who are your main competitors?

We have competitors in two distinct industries - education and professional marketplaces. From the latter category, recruitment platforms, freelance job sites whereby the former category, e-learning sites, Blockchain code academies will suffer. That being said, these are operating as sole service providers while DiscoverBlockchain focuses on providing both e-learning and professional marketplaces for Blockchain Technology - which is a unique approach to solving an emerging problem.

Why would students, educators, contractors and entrepreneurs use DiscoverBlockchain instead of other platforms?
Creating a trusted environment

DiscoverBlockchain establishes a system that provides order in a leaderless, egalitarian system by using the innovations provided by distributed ledger technology to bring better organization to future thinkers and doers.

Learning vs. Application

Our primary goal is to address waste resulting from disorganization within and between Education & Business. Academia and the workplace are no longer working hand in hand to bridge the gap between learners and workers. We want to be the bridge and give the opportunity to students to progress into service providers within the platform.

Decentralized Talent

Most Blockchain development occurs over the Internet, relying on the same strengths that are provided by remote learning and freelancing. In our solution, we integrate the remote learning and freelancing models into a single, reciprocal ecosystem.

What is the minimum contribution?

Minimum 50 USD or ETH/BTC/LTC/BCH/DASH/ZCASH/Ripple/EUR/GBP/CHF equivalent.

Can I participate in the Crowdsale if I’m from the U.S.?

All U.S. citizens/residents can't participate in the Crowdsale.
All Non-U.S. citizens do not have to be accredited to participate in the Crowdsale.

Can can I pay in US Dollars or Euros, British Pounds or Swiss Francs ?

Yes, we accept payments in USD, EUR, GBP & CHF. We will provide you with wire information for our bank accounts.

What is the ETH price of 1 DiscoverBlockchain Token (DSC)?

For the Crowdsale, the price of one DSC will be issued starting from USD $0.05 each (Private Pre-Sale price), all the way up to USD $0.1 each (Public Main Sale). It is not tied directly to ETH price and the conversion is based on the fixed price at the moment of DSC token Smart Contract generation.

What is soft-cap and hard-cap?

Soft-cap is 60000000 DSC (5 mil USD) and hard-cap is 360000000 DSC (30 mil USD).

When does Crowdsale start?

First stage of the Crowdsale will start on July 15th (Private Pre-Sale)
Next stage of the Crowdsale will start on September 1st (Public Pre-Sale)
Main Public Crowdsale will start on October 1st.

When does Crowdsale end?

The Crowdsale will end on September 30th or when we hit our hard-cap of $30m.

How many tokens will there be?

500 000 000 tokens in total (total supply), with 360 000 000 for Crowdsale.
Unsold tokens from the Crowdsale will be used for platform rewards system (early adopters rewards, affiliate system, partner companies bonuses). No new tokens will ever be created.

What documents do I need in order to pass KYC?

We’ll need a copy of your passport or ID and your portrait picture. We reserve the right to request additional documents if it's necessary.

How long does it take to get KYC verified?

It usually takes less than 12 hours to get KYC verified. However, you can still contribute and purchase your tokens in the meantime.

When will I get my DSC tokens?

If you're using ETH payments then your tokens will be issued few minutes after your purchase. Our ERC20 Smart Contract automatically sends tokens based on ETH payment amount.
If you're buying via any other method of payment (USD, EUR, GBP, CHF, BTC/LTC/BCH/DASH/ZCASH/Ripple), typical waiting period is 12-24 hours, because it's a manual approval process.

What is DiscoverBlockchain Token and why is it a utility token?

On-platform, users and DiscoverBlockchain partners will transact using the DiscoverBlockchain Coin (DSC) which is a utility token built on the Ethereum Blockchain. The creation of DSC is accompanied by Ethereum-built Smart Contracts that autonomously execute data and value exchange. For DiscoverBlockchain, DSC and Smart Contracts replace fiat currency and privately administered code respectively. DSC token is not a security token because token owners do not have voting rights as shareholders, share revenue or receive any dividends. There is no expectation of profit from the DSC token. The DSC Token passes the Howey test – check our legal section on the whitepaper to learn more about why we are a utility token.

Will the tokens be listed on exchanges? Can they be traded? When will they be listed in the exchanges?

We will be listing the DSC token in exchanges to spread its use and access to our platform. Subscribe to our newsletter to find out about the latest information on that matter.

Which wallets can be used to store Token?

The DiscoverBlockchain Token is an ERC20 token, and can be stored in ERC20 compatible wallets: any wallet that can store Ethereum is convenient. Notice: we do not accept wallets from online exchanges, we recommend MyEtherWallet or any hardware wallet (Ledger Nano S, Trezor).

What crypto-currencies & fiat currencies are accepted during the crowdsale?

ETH/BTC/LTC/BCH/DASH/ZCASH/Ripple as well as USD, EUR, GBP & CHF.

What will happen to tokens that aren't sold or not offered for Crowdsale?

Tokens that aren't sold or offered for Crowsale, will be put into a Platform Tokens fund and be used for further use as utility purposes (Early adopters rewards, affiliate system, partner companies bonuses).
